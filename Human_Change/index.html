HUMAN EVOLUTIONARY CHANGE

THE PERSON / THE PLACE / THE PURPOSE

Our lives on this planet are bound into a great purpose.


To update and enliven these three pillars – is to reconnect with our core humanity.

This is our CALLING

CALLING



Ask yourself, what does it CALL out of you that you live on a planet, that there is air, water, that we share this place with such a rich variety of other lives, that there are seasons and colour and the rich tapestry of life?

Our future has to be more than ‘sorting out’ pollution and erosion and destruction – it has to begin in recognizing the opportunity of life in this place and in this time.

We have lost our spiritual connection to life – and no amount of organizing and goal making about a different future will re-make that connection... until we listen to the CALLING and re-engage in our spiritual core.



RESPONSE



We are presented with responses about the big issues (global warming, pollution, loss of species...) and what ‘should’ be done (use less fossil fuels, reduce our reliance on plastic products…)...

…but inside the day-to-day minutes of living we can choose to acknowledge the sanctity and CALLING of life and the part we can play aligned to those subtle threads that nurture our inner world.

Until we really respond to the calling of our purpose we continue as disconnected lives. Ask yourself, what can disconnected lives do?



ACTION



Actions that are motivated by what is ‘wrong’ in the world will never make the world ‘right’. How many great individuals have pointed this out!

Conscious action joins the CALLING to the ACTION through the RESPONSES of our selves.

We become the change.

When we know the purpose – ‘why’ we do what we do – every moment of living is an opportunity to contribute meaningfully to a new world.

We are here to bring new ways, new compassion and new perception to the world.

A Practical Philosophy

The global challenges we face are vast and serious – who can doubt it – and yet the threads of such issues as pollution, global warming, regional conflict and the breakdown of the environment all lead to one place – ourselves and the way and level of our thinking.

We are on the edge of a new paradigm in the final hour. As the maxim goes, we cannot do more of the same and expect a different result – scientists, spiritual leaders and common sense all agree on this point.
