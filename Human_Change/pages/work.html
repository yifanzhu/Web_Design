The Work

When you ask yourself what is the core matter, the central focus and theme of living, does it not come to the fact that there are always the same three considerations? • We are humans • We are living on a planet • We have a purpose We call these three themes The Three Pillars of Living: the person, the place and the purpose. We cannot address environmental challenges when we are lost or angry or reacting to what is wrong in our own inner environment. We cannot help a future purpose if our own purpose is centered only on ‘sorting out the mess of the past’ and simply ‘surviving’. We cannot MAKE the world a better place using the means of coercion, pressure and regulation. Whichever way we combine the influence of these three pillars, one cannot go forward without all three integrating principles playing a part together. This is ‘A Training for the Future’! All the writings and videos and the tutorials and practices developed in this work center on one or all of The Three Pillars. They support a new platform for the human race – a new basis for living that keeps us on line with our purpose and in harmony with our environment. Let’s begin now!
